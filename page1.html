Family Grid book
10x10 Accordian Fold book

I created an accordion-fold flip book that shares information about my family. Throughout this project, I strengthened my content development skills and improved my understanding of spacing and layout while working within a structured grid system. The assignment required me to write several paragraphs about each family member and then carefully organize the text so it fit cleanly into my grid design. I used InDesign to create my grid and layout for my booklet. After finalizing the layout, I printed five 10x10 pages and taped them together to form the full accordion flip book.
