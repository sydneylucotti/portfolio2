About Us

Welcome to Sugar Shot, where indulgence meets creativity in the heart of Uptown Normal. Our dessert bar is a vibrant, cozy haven for those who appreciate the finer things in life without the stuffy fine dining atmosphere. At Sugar Shot, we’re all about high-quality, scratch-made desserts paired with the perfect drink, whether you’re in the mood for a classic cocktail, a glass of wine, or a fun non-alcoholic concoction.

Our menu is a delightful mix of traditional treats and exciting twists on classic flavors, allcrafted with love and a touch of irreverence. We know that life’s sweetest moments deserve to be celebrated, whether it’s a first date, a milestone event, or just a spontaneous night out with friends. And if you’re an adult who believes that dessert is even better with a glass of champagne or beer, you’ve found your new favorite spot.

Inspired by the rich history of Uptown Normal and the unique flair of local artists, our space is designed to be welcoming and inclusive, a place where everyone feels at home. We also offer a party space for those special occasions that call for something a little ...extra. At Sugar Shot, we’re not just serving desserts and drinks—we’re creating experiences, one sweet shot at a time. Come join us and discover your new favorite indulgence.
