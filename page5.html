2040 Olympics
Colabrative Project

I collaborated with classmates to create logos, mockups, and design concepts for a hypothetical Olympic Games hosted in Karaj, Iran. This project strengthened my communication, teamwork, and research skills, and gave me valuable experience working collaboratively toward a shared design goal. I contributed heavily to the background research, initial logo mockups, mood board development, and the creation of our design guide. I began with sketches and early concepts, shared and discussed them with my team, and then helped combine our ideas into the final logos, mockups, and visual assets. We used Various platforms to create our mockups and branding, such as InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects. We concluded the project by presenting our work to classmates, our instructor, and a regional designer from the region we were assigned.
