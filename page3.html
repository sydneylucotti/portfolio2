Zine Of Resistance
4x5 Zine

I created a zine featuring content from the symbols of resistance I designed in a previous project. I created my symbols on Illustrator, then converted the symbols and research into InDesign outline. Since this was my first time making a zine, it helped me further develop my design skills and gain a better understanding of layout and sequencing. For this assignment, I first composed a digital outline of the zine and then printed a physical mockup. The final piece included information and visual symbols that represented the theme of reusing and recycling old vinyl material to create new vinyl. I assembled the zine by cutting, folding, and arranging the printed layout into its final form.
