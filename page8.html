Lithographs
8x11 Paper

I created three lithography prints to showcase my illustration skills and artistic style. Lithography is a printmaking technique where an image is drawn with a greasy medium on a flat stone or metal plate. The surface is chemically treated so ink sticks only to the drawing, and then paper is pressed onto it to create multiple prints. This process requires precision and allows for experimentation with textures, lines, and shading. Each print reflects careful attention to detail and composition. After printing, the pieces are finished by drying, trimming, and preparing them for display. Through this project, I combined traditional printmaking techniques with my own style to create visually appealing and polished works.
