Sigma Sigma Sigma Merchandise
Apparel

As the Marketing Manager for Sigma Sigma Sigma in 2024, I created and executed a wide range of designs that represented our chapter’s identity and events. In this role, I collaborated closely with chapter officers, event coordinators, and fellow members to ensure all apparel and promotional materials aligned with the sorority’s vision and were appealing to the sisterhood. I leveraged my graphic design skills to develop custom apparel concepts, refine designs based on feedback, and ensure high-quality final products. I worked directly with apparel vendors such as Fresh Prints and University Tees, communicating design needs, negotiating pricing, and managing order approval processes. By coordinating production timelines and staying aligned with chapter expectations, I ensured each design was both on-brand and highly desired by members.
